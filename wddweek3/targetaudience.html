Target Audience

Audience

The audience for this website will be people who like to spend time outdoors and at community events. It will be geared toward families, but will be a good resource for anyone that wants to get out.

Personas

Persona 1: Julia is a young mom, age 27. She is a middle class, work at home mom with an annual household income of 60,000-70,000 a year. Julia likes to get her two young kids out of the house often and avoid screen time. She values time with her family, a healthy lifestyle, an a feelin of community. She is very active and enjoys being social.

Persona 2: Ben is a middle aged dad with 5 kids. He has an office job and makes 75,000 a year. His wife is a stay at home mom. He likes to go out cycling after work and enjoys getting his famliy outdoorson the weekend. Ben likes four-wheeling and mountain biking in his spare time. Because Ben has limited time with his work, he likes to plan activites ahead of time.
